Footer

The footer shows copyright information and social links.

Example

Connect with us here!
Email LinkedIn Contact

Copyright@2025 Sri Harshita. All Rights Reserved

Code

<footer class="text-center p-4">
  <img src="../logo/color logo.png" alt="Logo" style="max-width: 80px;" class="mb-3" />
  <h5>Connect with us here!</h5>
  <div class="d-flex gap-4 justify-content-center">
    <a href="#">Email</a>
    <a href="#">LinkedIn</a>
    <a href="#">Contact</a>
  </div>
  <p class="text-muted mt-3 small">Copyright@2025 Sri Harshita. All Rights Reserved</p>
</footer>

Usage

  • Place the footer at the bottom of every page after main content.
  • Include links for communication and basic ownership information.

Accessibility Considerations

Ensure adequate contrast for text and links. Use semantic HTML elements (like <footer>) for better accessibility and SEO. Include alt text for logos.
